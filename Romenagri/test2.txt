maarchu

ru_drama_daeewi (_aa_mgla_m : "e_Rudrama" "e_Devi") kaaka_teeyula wa_msha_mloa _owka _dh_riwa_taaragaa waeligina ma_haaraa_nni[1]. kaaka_teeya wa_mshamunaku gowppa paeeru prakhyaa_tulani _taechchipaettina weerawani_ta. bhaara_ta_daeesha chari_traloa raajyaalanu _aeelina ma_haaraa_nnulaloa ru_drama_daeewi _owkaru. _eemae _asalu paeeru ru_draa_mba. kaaka_teeya ga_nnapa_ti_daeewudu, paalakudaina jayaapasaeenaani soa_dareema_nnulaina naara_mbha, paeeraa_mbhalanu wiwaa_ha maadinaadu, weeri mu_d_dula kumaar_tae ru_drama_daeewi. chaeebroalu shaasana_m _deeni guri_mchi _taeliyajaees_tu_m_di. _eemae _ta_mdri ga_nnapa_ti_daeewuniki pu_tra sa_m_taana_m laee_du. _a_m_duwalana ru_draa_mbanu _tana kumaarudilaa pae_mchukowni ru_dra_daeewudani naamakara_nna_m chaeesaadu[2]. ga_nnapa_ti_daeewudu _tana kumaar_tae ru_drama_daeewini nirawa_dyapura (nida_dawoalu ) praa_m_taanni paalis_tunna _toorpu chaalvrukyudaina weerabha_drudiki _ichchi wiwaa_ha_m chaeeshaadu. ru_drama_daeewiki _i_d_daru kumaar_taelu. pae_d_dakumaar_tae mummadamma. _eemae ma_haa_daeewuni bhaarya. weeri pu_trudaee pra_taaparu_drudu. ru_dramaa_mba pra_taaparu_druni _da_t_ta_ta _teesukowni yuwaraajugaa pattaabhixaeeka_m chaeesi_m_di. _annama_daeewudu _anaee _tammudu _u_mdaeewaadani s_thaanika gaa_tha. bas_tar raajya chiwari paalaka wa_msha_mwaaru _annama_daeewuni _tama wa_mshakar_tagaa chaeppukunnaaru. ru_drama_daeewi rae_mdawa kumaar_tae ruyyamma.

jeewi_ta wishaeexaalu

kaaka_teeyulaloa _agraga_nnyudaina ga_nnapa_ti_daeewuni _taruwaa_ta 1269 loa ru_drama_daeewi ' ru_drama_haaraaju ' biru_da_m_toa kaaka_teeya ma_haasaamraajya si_m_haasanaanni _a_dhixti_mchi_m_di. _ayi_taee _owka ma_hilvra paalakuraalu kaawata_m _oarwalaeeni _anaeekama_m_di saama_m_tulu _tirugubaatu chaeeshaaru. _a_daeesamaya_mloa naellooru paa_mdyula ki_m_da, waee_mgi praa_m_ta_m gow_mkaraaju mow_dati narasi_m_hudi ki_m_daki waelvrlvrinaayi. paakanaati kaayas_tha _a_mba_daeewudu, kalvri_mga narasi_m_huni kumaarudu weerabhaanudu _tirugubaatlu chaeeshaaru. ru_drama _tana saeenaanula_toa kalisi _ee _tirugubaatlanannitinee wijayawa_m_ta_mgaa _a_nnachiwaeesi_m_di. ru_dramaa_mba _ae_durkownna _da_mdayaa_tralannitiloakee _daeewagiri yaa_dawaraajula _da_mdayaa_tra _a_ti pae_d_da_di, keelakamaina_di. yaa_dawaraaju ma_haa_daeewudu _oarugallunu muttadi_mchaadu, _ayi_taee ru_drama_daeewi yaa_dawulanu _oadi_mchi, _daeewagiri _durga_m warakoo _tarimi kowtti_m_di. waeeraee_daari laeeni ma_ha_daeewudu sa_m_dhiki _digiwachchi, yu_d_dha pari_haara_mgaa moodukoatla suwar_nnaalu chaelli_mchaadu. ru_drama_daeewi yowkka shaiwama_ta guruwu wishwaeeshwara shiwasha_mbu. ga_nnapa_ti _daeewuniki, rae_mdawa pra_taaparu_druniki koodaa _eeyanaee guruwu. ru_drama _taanaee swaya_mgaa kaayas_ta raajya_mpai _daadi chaeesinatlu _taelus_toa_m_di. cha_m_dupatla shaasana_m _aa_dhaara_mgaa kaayas_ta _a_mba_daeewuni_toa jarigina yu_d_dhaalaloanaee mara_nni_mchinatlu chari_trakaarulu bhaawis_tunnaaru. ru_drama_daeewiki gala _i_tara biru_dulu: raayagajakaeesari, ghatoa_dh_ri_ti.

prakhyaa_ta pa_thikudu maarkoa poaloa chhainaa _daeeshamunu_mdi _tirigiwaelvr_too _dakxi_nnabhaara_ta_daeeshamu sa_m_darshi_mchi ru_drama_daeewi guri_mchi, _aamae paalana guri_mchi ba_huwi_dhamula powgidaadu. moatupalli raeewunu_mdi kaaka_teeyula samu_dra wyaapaaramu guri_mchi koodaa wiwaramugaa wraashaadu[3].

raa_nnee ru_drama_daeewi guri_mchi manaku _taelisi_m_di

raa_nnee ru_drama _daeewi guri_mchi manaku _taelisina_daanikannaa _taeliyani_daee _aekkuwa. _aamae janma sa_mwa_tsara_m _taeliya_du. _ujjaayi_mpugaa _oo_hi_mchadaaniki weelu_m_di _ani kaaka_teeya yugamu gra_m_tha_mloa lakxmeera_mjana_m raashaaru. ru_drama_daeewi guri_mchi chari_trakaarulakoo _taeliyani wixayaalu _aennoa _unnaayi.
ru_drama_daeewi guri_mchi shaasanaa_dhaaraalu

    ru_drama_daeewi kree.sha. 1261 maarchi 25wa _taee_deena _ippati _aa_m_dhrapra_daeesh, gu_mtooru jillaa k_rix_nnaa na_di _dakxi_nna _teeraanunna ma_m_dada_m graama_mloa, raajaguruwu wishwaeeshwara shiwaachaarya samakxa_mloa puttinaroaju waeedukalu jarupukownnatlu _akkada _dowrikina shaasana_m _taeliyajaees_toa_m_dani _i_di warakaee paeerkownnaanu. _daaniwalla koodaa puttina_taee_dee _taelus_toa_m_daee _tappa sa_mwa_tsara_m kaa_du.
    kree.sha. 1257 naati ju_t_tiga shaasana_m prakaara_m _aamae nida_dawoaluku chae_m_dina chaalvrukya weerabha_druni pae_mdlaadi_m_di.
    kree.sha. 1259 nu_mchaee _aamae _ta_mdriki sa_hakaris_too paripaalanaanubhawaanni sa_mpaa_di_mchi_m_di.
    1262 sa_mwa_tsara_m nu_mchaee _aamae swa_ta_m_tra_mgaa paripaali_mchata_m praara_mbhi_mchi_m_di.
    kree.sha. 1268loa naellooru jillaa mu_t_tukooru praa_m_ta_m warakoo chowchchukowchchina weeraraajaee_m_dra choalvrunni _oadi_mchi, _aa bhoobhaagaanni sow_m_ta_m chaeesukow_m_di. _ayinappatikee kaayas_ta janniga _daeewudu gu_mtooru jillaa palnaaduloani _durgi graama_mloa kree.sha. 1269wa sa_mwa_tsara_mloa waeeyi_mchina shaasana_m ru_drama_daeewini pattoa_dh_ri_ti (yuwaraa_nni) gaanaee paeerkow_m_di.

_digwijaya_mgaa paalana

_aamae _tana shak_tisaamar_thyaala_toa pra_ti _owkkarinee maeppi_mchi _digwijaya_mgaa paalanaa wyawa_haaraalanu nirwa_hi_mchaaru. prakhyaa_ta kaaka_teeya wa_mshaaniki chae_m_dina ru_drama_daeewi _oarugallu (naeeti wara_mgallu) raaja_dhaanigaa paripaali_mchaaru. kree.sha.1262 nu_mchi 1289 waraku sumaaru 27 sa_mwa_tsaraala paatu chakkati paripaalana chaeeshaaru. mana_daeesha_mloa ma_hilvraapaalakulu chaalaa _aru_du. raa_nnee ru_drama_daeewiki kow_d_dikaala_mmu_m_daee su_doora_mloa _unna dhilleeni rajiyaa sul_taanaa _anaee ma_hilvra paripaali_mchaaru.prabhuwargaalaku chae_m_dina waaru s_treeparipaalana _ixta_m laeeka _aamaenu paalakuraaligaa _a_mgeekari_mchaka _tu_damutti_mchaaru. pramukha _itaaliyan yaa_trikudaina maarkoapoaloa ru_drama_daeewi raajyaanni sa_m_darshi_mchi, _aamae paripaalana, _dhairyasaa_hasaalanu kowniyaadaadu. _aamae puruxula _dus_tulu _dhari_mchi nirbhaya_mgaa, sunaayaasa_mgaa gurraala swaaree chaeesaeewaarani paeerkownnaadu. naati shaasanaalaloa ru_drama_daeewi ru_dra_daeewa ma_haaraajugaa keer_ti_mchabadi_m_di. rajiyaa sul_taanaa laagaa ru_drama_daeewi koodaa _tana _ta_mdri paalanaa kaala_mloani mukhyamaina naayakula wya_tiraeeka_tanu wijayawa_m_ta_mgaa _a_nnichiwaeesi_m_di. ru_drama_daeewi, _aamae manumadaina pra_taaparu_drudi paalanaloa chaelaraeegina _anaeeka saama_m_ta _tirugubaatlanu niya_m_tri_mchadaaniki palu charyalu _teesukunnaaru.

raaj_yni ru_drama _daeewini goorchi raas_too prasi_d_dha chari_trakaarulu malla_mpalli soamashaeekhara sharma, daaktaru naeelatooru wae_mkatarama_nnayya _itlaa _abhiwar_nni_mchaaru.

    _taelugu waarini paali_mchina _daeesha paripaalakulaloa ru_drama_daeewi nissa_m_daee_ha_mgaa ma_haaghana_ta chae_m_dina wyak_ti. raaja_dharma wi_dhulanu _aamae nirwa_hi_mchina _teeruwalana _ta_mdri _aamaeku prasaa_di_mchina puruxanaamamu, 'ru_dra_daeewudu' _anni wi_dhamula saar_thakamaina_di. prajalu _aamaenu ru_dra_daeewa ma_haaraaju _ani piluchukunaeewaaru. _daeesha paripaalanaloa _aamae chai_tanyawa_m_tamaina paa_tra wa_hi_mchi_m_di. _dhairya saa_hasamulu, wikramamu kala yoa_dhuraalu _awadamaee kaaka, _aamae gowppa wyoo_ha _ta_m_traj_ynuraalu. _aamae raajarika_m chaeesina kaala_mloa _taruchoo yu_d_dhamula _alajadi kaliginaa, _aamae prajalu sa_m_tuxtulu, sa_mpree_tulu _ayi sukhi_mchaaru.

ru_drama_daeewi paalana prajaara_mjakamu

ru_drama_daeewi paalana prajaara_mjakamai bhaasilli_m_di. shaa_m_ti sus_thira_tala_toa wiraajilli_m_di. kree. sha. 1000 nu_mchi 1323 waraku _daa_daapu moodu sha_taab_daala paatu _taelugu naeelanaeeli_m_di kaaka_teeya wa_msha_m. weerikaala_mloanaee _trili_mga, _aa_m_dhra pa_daalaku _ar_tha_m, paramaar_tha_m _aeerpaddaayi. kaaka_teeya wa_msha_mloa sap_tama chakrawar_ti _ayina ga_nnapa_ti _daeewudu _a_tya_m_ta paraakramawa_m_tudu, raajanee_ti koawi_dudu. _eeyanaku _i_d_daru koo_tulvrlu. ma_hilvralu raajyaa_dhikaaraaniki _anar_hulanna _appati kaalaparis_thi_tulaku _talowggina _aayana _tana koo_turu ru_drama_daeewini kumaarudigaa pae_mchaadu. _anni wi_dyaloo naeerpi_mchaadu. ga_nnapa_ti _daeewudu ru_dramanu raajapra_tini_dhigaa prakati_mchinappudu _aamae wayasu pa_dnaalugaeelvrlaee. _appati nu_mchi _aamae _ta_mdri chaatubiddagaa paalana saagi_mchi_m_di. ru_drama_daeewi 1261 praa_m_ta_m nu_mchee swa_ta_m_tra_mgaa paripaali_mchinatlu kanabadu_tu_m_di. kownni shaasanaalloa 1279 waraku pattaabhixak_turaalu kaalaee_daeemoa _anaee bhaawa_m kaligi_mchaee raa_talunnaayi
paalanaakaalama_m_taa yu_d_dhaala_toanaee

ru_drama _daeewi paalanaakaalama_m_taa yu_d_dhaala_toanaee gadichi_m_di. _towlu_ta s_tree paripaalananu, s_tree _a_dhikaaraanni sa_hi_mchalaeeni saama_m_tula nu_mchi _daayaa_dula nu_mchi _aamaeku _teewra pra_tighatana _ae_durayyi_m_di. _ta_mdri ga_nnapa_ti _daeewuni kaala_mloa saama_m_tulugaa _unna raajulu ru_drama si_m_haasana_m _a_dhixti_mchagaanaee _ae_duru _tirigaaru. _tirugubaatlu laeewa_deeshaaru. _ayi_taee _ee wipa_t_tulanni_mtinee _aamae samar_thawa_m_ta_mgaa _ae_durkow_m_di. paripaalanaa _dakxa_taloa naeerpari _ayina raa_nnee ru_drama waari _asooyanu _a_nnichi waeesi_m_di. _dakxi_nnaa_dini paali_mchaee choalvrulu, maraathaa praa_m_ta yaa_dawulanu samar_thawa_m_ta_mgaa _ae_durkowni raajyaanni kaapaadina yoa_dhuraalu raa_nnee ru_drama. _daeewagiri ma_haa_daeewudu _aenimi_di lakxala ma_haasainya_m_toa ru_dramapaiki _da_mdae_t_ti wachchaadu. ma_ha_daeewunipai pa_diroajulaku paigaa jarigina bheekara poaraata_mloa ru_drama pra_tyakxa_mgaa paalgownna_di. _tana _apaara shak_ti saamar_thyaala_toa _apara bha_drakaalvrilaa wij_ri_mbhi_mchi_m_di. _aamae _tana chirakaala pra_tyar_thi ma_ha_daeewudni _aa yu_d_dha_mloa mattikaripi_mchi moodu koatla ba_mgaaru wara_haalanu pari_haara_mgaa gra_hi_mchi_m_di. sha_truwunu _aar_thika_mgaa chaawu _daebbakowtti malvrlee _talae_t_taku_mdaa chaeesi_m_di. ru_drama maeenamaama, ga_nnapa_ti _daeewuni baawamari_di, jayaapanaayudu koodaa ru_drama wijaya_mloa _toatpaatunichchina weerudu. _i_tadu ga_nnapa_ti _daeewudi kudibhujamu wa_mtiwaadu. _i_tadu 'gee_ta ra_tnaawalvri', 'n_ri_tyara_tnaawalvri' gra_m_thaalu rachi_mchaadu. ga_nnapa_ti_daeewudi sarwasainyaa_dyakxudu, gaja_dalvraa_dhipa_ti, kammanaadu _diwiseema raajyapaalaka raaju. ru_drama _a_mgarakxakulu, ba_m_dhuwargeeyulaina kamma _durjaya wa_mshas_thulu. _aerranaayudu, pow_t_tinaayudu, ru_dranaayudu, pinnaru_dranaayudu, _aekkinaayudu, kowmminaayudu mow_dalagu ba_m_dhuwargeeyulu ru_drama_daeewi yu_d_da wijayaalloa _toadu nilichaaru.ru_drama jaripina poaraataalanni_mtiloa _aamaeku baasatagaa nilichina waaru raeecharla prasaa_di_tyudu, ru_dranaayakudu, janniga_daeewudu, _tripuraa_m_takudu.
weerabha_druni_toa wiwaa_ha_m

pa_dnaalugawa yaeetanaee paalanaa paggaalu chaeepattina ru_dramaku _irawai _ai_doa yaeeta nida_dawoalu raajaina chaalvrukya weerabha_druni_toa wiwaa_hamai_m_di. raa_nni ru_drama_daeewi bhar_ta chaalvrukya weerabha_dhrudu gouda kulaaniki chae_m_dina chaalvrukya raajulu. weeriki _i_d_daru koo_tulvrlu mummadamma, ruyyamma. _tanaku maga sa_m_taana_m laeekapoawada_m_toa ru_drama _tana pae_d_da kumaar_tae mummadamma kumaarudaina pra_taaparu_drudni _da_t_ta_ta _teesukuni yuwaraajugaa pattaabhixaeeka_m chaeesi_m_di. wi_dhi praa_tikoolya_m chaee_ta ru_drama _daeewi bhar_ta chaalvrukya weerabha_drudu kree.sha. 1266 naatikaee m_ri_tichae_m_dinatlu _aayana _talli _u_daya ma_haa_daeewi paalakowllu shaasana_mloa _u_m_di. bhar_ta mara_nnaaniki sameepa kaala_mloanaee ru_drama_daeewiki marowka _teerani _du_hhkha_m kaligi_m_di. w_ri_d_dhudaina ga_nnapa_ti _daeewa chakrawar_ti 1267loa shiwasaayujya_m chae_m_di _aamaenu nissa_haayuraalni chaeeshaadu. ru_dramaku _i_d_daru koo_tulvrlaee kaaka, _aellana _daeewuni bhaarya koodaa ru_drama_daeewi _tanaya _ani prak_ri_tashaasana_m chaebu_toa_m_di.
ru_drama_daeewi paalanaloa

raa_nnee ru_drama _tana_daina shaililoa, _aru_daina ree_tiloa paalana saagi_mchi_m_di. prajalanu, mukhya_mgaa ma_hilvralanu _aamae _ar_tha_m chaeesukunnattugaa _aee _i_tara raajuloo _ar_tha_m chaeesukoalaee_du. ru_dra_daeewudi roopa_mloa _unna ru_drama pattoa_dh_ri_ti _a_mtaee raajapra_tini_dhigaa baa_dhya_talu sweekari_mchina _ana_m_tara_m _daeeshama_m_taa kaliya _tirigi_m_di. prajaasamasyalu pra_tyakxa_mgaa _taelusuku_m_di. yuwaraajugaa _aamae _aekkadi samasyalu _akkadaee parixkari_mchi_m_di. raajya_mloa _owka choata _owka _talli kaanpuloanaee kannu mooyada_m choosi ru_drama _talladilli_m_di. _owkka kxa_nna_m koodaa _aalasya_m chaeeyaku_mdaa saa_hasoapaee_tamaina nir_nnaya_m _teesuku_m_di. graamagraamaana prasoo_ti wai_dyashaalalu katti_mchamani _aa_daeshi_mchi_m_di. _ikapai raajya_mloa prasawa samaya_mloa _owkka maa_t_rimoor_ti koodaa m_ri_tyuwaa_ta padadaaniki weellaee_du _ani prakati_mchi_m_di.

suwishaala ma_haasaamraajyaanni

mana ru_drama _asamaana paraakramashaali. kaaka_teeya paalakula waibhawaaniki samunna_ta kaee_tana_m. ru_dramma bhujashak_ti, _dheeyuk_ti_toa sha_truwula paalita si_m_haswapnamai_m_di. _a_m_ta_hhsha_truwulu, bayati sha_truwula kutralu, ku_ta_m_traalaennoa samar_tha_mgaa _ae_durkownna weerawani_ta. saamraajyaanni _dakxi_nnaana _tamilvranaaduloani ka_mchi nu_mchi _u_t_taraana cha_t_teesghad bas_tar seema waraku, padamarana baeda_danaadu nu_mchi _toorpuna samu_dra_m waraku, _eeshaanya_mloa ga_mjaa_m _a_mtaee _owrissaa waraku kaaka_teeya saamraajyaanni wis_tari_mpajaeesi_m_di. balawa_m_tula_daee raajyamanna ma_dhyayugaalloanaee ru_drama _dakxi_nnaapa_tha_mloa suwishaala ma_haasaamraajyaanni naelakowlpi_m_di. _aamae saa_hasaanikee, _dheera_twaanikee, _taeguwakoo, paalanaa _dakxa_takoo maarupaeerugaa nilichi_m_di. _taela_mgaa_nna ma_hilvra paalanaa patimanu, mana jaa_ti khyaa_tini wishwawikhyaa_ta_m chaeesi_m_di.
saama_m_ta raajula_toa paalana

goana gannaaraeddi saama_m_ta raajula_toa paalanaloa, _anaeekama_m_di _unna saama_m_tulaloa, bu_d_dhapura_m ma_haaraaju goana bu_d_dhaaraeddi _owkadu. _a_taniki kumaarudu goana gannaaraeddi _ta_mdrilaagaee kaaka_teeyawa_msha weeraabhimaani. ma_haaraaju goana bu_d_dhaaraeddi sa_m_taana_m gannaaraeddi, withalaraeddi. wayoabhaara_m paerigi _awasaana_dashaki chaeerina ma_haaraaju goana bu_d_dhaaraeddi _tammudu lakumayaaraeddini pilichi, _tana pae_d_da kowduku gannaaraeddi paeeru_toa raajyapaalana chaeyyamani, _a_tanu pae_d_dayyaaka raajya_m _a_tanikaee _appagi_mchi,waeerowka nagara_m paripaali_mchukowmmani chaeppi kannumoos_taadu. lakumayya pasiwaadaina gannaaraeddi paeeru_toa raajya_m chaees_too, _aa pillalni wi_dyaabhyaasa_m koasa_m _oarugallu pa_mpis_taadu._tanaee raajulaa chaelaama_nni _awu_taadu. ru_dramma peetha_m _aekkaaka, '_owka _aada_daani moachaee_tineelvrlvru _taagaalaa' _ani _hu_mkari_mchi swaa_ta_m_tyra_m prakati_mchukoawaalani nir_nnayi_mchuku_mtaadu. _tanalaagaee s_treepaalanaloa _tala_daachukoawadaaniki _ixtapadani miga_taa saama_m_taraajula_toa, ru_dramma_daeewiki warusaku _annalaina _hari_hara_daeewulu, muraari_daeewulu, _i_tara sha_truraajula_toa kappa_m chaelli_mchaku_mdaa _aapaees_taaru. goana gannaaraeddi _daeesha_mloa jarugu_tunna pari_nnaamaalu gamani_mchi kow_m_tama_m_di weerulanu _tayaaruchaeesi, ru_drama_daeewini kalus_taadu. _taama_m_taa gaja_dow_mgalagaa _awa_taaramae_t_ti, raajya_mloa chaelaraeegu_tunna _akramaalanu paara_droali, _tirugubaatlanu _a_nnichiwaees_taamani, _a_m_duku _anuj_yna _iwwamani koaru_taadu. ru_dramma_daeewi saraeena_mtu_m_di. gannaaraeddi nallamala _adawulaloa _owka paadubadina _durgaanni baaguchaeeyi_mchi, _tana saeena_toa ra_hasya_mgaa _akkada _u_mtoo _a_dharmanirmoolana_m chaees_too_mtaadu.

_usmaaniyaa yooniwarshiteeloa _taelugu reesaerch skaalar gaa panichaees_tunna ta_mgutoori sai_dulu kaaka_teeya ru_drama_daeewi mara_nnaaniki sa_mba_m_dhi_mchina chaari_trika _aa_dhaaraala koasa_m _anwaeexi_mchasaagaadu. _i_m_duloa bhaaga_mgaanaee munugoaduloa mattiloa koorukupoayina shaasanaanni gur_ti_mchi puraawas_tu shaakhaa_dhikaarula sa_haaya_m_toa waeliki_teeyi_mchi _a_di ru_drama_daeewi mara_nnashaasana_mgaa gur_ti_mchaaru. wiroa_dhanaama sa_mwa_tsara_m _dwaa_dashi roajuna _a_mtaee 1289wa sa_mwa_tsara_m, nawa_mbaru 27wa _taee_deena raa_nnee ru_drama_daeewi weeramara_nna_m pow_m_dinatlugaa shaasana_mpai likhi_mchinatlu waelladai_m_di.

_taela_mgaa_nnaloa nallamala _adawulu _hai_daraabaa_d nu_mchi 135 kiloameetarla _doora_mloa ma_haboobnagar jillaaloani mannanooruloa praara_mbhamawu_taayi. _akkadinu_mchi 50kiloameetarla _doora_mloa _u_mdaee bouraapura_m (bhramaraa_mbapura_m) mee_dugaa kaalinadakana maroa pa_di kiloameetarla _doora_m prayaa_nnis_taee maeedimalkal _anaee chae_mchupae_mta shiwaaruloa _durgama _ara_nnya_mloa _owka shilaashaasana_m wu_m_di. maeedimalkalloa (_owkappati maeedima la_mkalu _anaee graama_mloa) _ee shaasana_m waeeyi_mchina roaju kree.sha.1290 phibrawari 20 (wiroa_dhi paalgu_nna shukra) naatikaee mallikaarjuna _daeewaalaya_m, _daaniki _anuba_m_dha_mgaa kalu matha_m _u_mdaeewi. _aalaya_mloa shree parwa_ta shree swaya_mbhu shreeli_mga chakrawar_ti shree mallikaarjuna ma_haali_mga_m _anaee _daeewudu_mdaeewaadu. kaara_nnaalu _aeewoa gaanee _aa _aalaya_m, matha_m kow_m_takaala_m niraa_dara_nnaku gurainawi. _alaa jarugakooda_dani pra_taaparu_druni ma_haa saama_m_tudu chaeraku bowllayaraeddi 1290 phibrawari 20 naati sooryagra_ha_nna kaalamuna swaami waari _a_mgara_mga bhoagaalaku, kalu mathaaniki _anaeeka _daanaalu chaeeshaadu. _aa wiwaraalu shilaa shaasana_m chiwarloa _unnaayi.1289loa nawa_mbaru 27na _aamae weeramara_nna_m chae_m_dinattu nalgow_mda jillaa nakiraeekal praa_m_ta_mloani cha_m_dupatla shaasana_m spaxta_mchaees_tunna_di.bhaara_ti maee 1974 sa_mchikaloa chari_tra parishoa_dhakudu peewee parabra_hmashaas_trigaaru _ee wixayaalanu charchis_too sawiwara_mgaa wyaasa_m raayata_m_toa _ee sa_mga_ti prapa_mchaaniki _taelisi_m_di. maeedimalkal loa  _dowrikina kow_t_ta shaasana_m(cha_m_dupatla shaasana_m)loa paeerkownna ru_drama mara_nna _taee_dee nijamaee _ani nir_dhaaris_toa_m_di.
